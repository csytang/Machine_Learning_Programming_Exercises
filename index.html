W2_Linear Regression with Multiple Variables

In this exercise, logistic regression along with gradient descent is implemented and applied to two different datasets. Specifically, the logistic regression model is employed to predict whether a student gets admitted into a university. With regularized logistic regression, the real-life dataset from a fabrication plant is used to assess the quality.
